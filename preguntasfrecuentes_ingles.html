Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept returns?

Yes, all of our items can be returned or exchanged within a maximum period of 30 days from the purchase.

Shipping costs for international returns will be paid by the customers.

*Returns are accepted as long as the products are new and not used.

How long does the refund take?

The refund can take up to 7 business days, depending on the bank.

Refunds will be made on the same credit card.

Do you sell eBook?

Yes, at The Book Palace we offer books in any format, including eBooks.

Where's my order?

You will recive a tracking code in the confirmation email, in which you can see the exact location of your order, its estimated delivery date and the delivery method.
